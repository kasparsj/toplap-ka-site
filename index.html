👋 Who we are

We are a live coding community based in Karlsruhe, Germany. We want to meet regularily in the physical and virtual space, organize workshops and concerts and connect with other live coding communities all around the globe.

📝 Get in touch

Want to join us? We're currently mostly organized via a Signal chat group. Send us an email with a quick introduction of yourself and what you do and we'll add you! If you have any other inquries also don't hesitate and write.
